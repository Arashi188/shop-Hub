Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025

30-Day Return Window

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to request a return for most products. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with all tags attached.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned: perishable goods, custom products, personal care items, gift cards, and clearance items.

How to Initiate a Return

1. Log into your account and go to Orders
2. Select the item you wish to return
3. Follow the instructions to print a return label
4. Package your item securely and ship it back

Refunds

Once we receive your return, we will inspect the item and process your refund within 5-7 business days. Refunds will be issued to your original payment method.

Return Shipping

If the return is due to our error (wrong or defective item), we will cover shipping costs. Otherwise, return shipping is the customer's responsibility.

Need Help?

Contact our returns team at returns@luxe.com or call +234 812 345 6789
