Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with LUXE

How long does shipping take?
Domestic shipping within Nigeria takes 2-5 business days. International shipping takes 7-14 business days depending on location.
What is your return policy?
We offer 30-day returns on all unused items in original packaging. Contact support to initiate a return.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs calculated at checkout.
How do I track my order?
You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Track via our website or carrier's portal.
Are your products sustainable?
Absolutely! We're committed to using organic, recycled, and ethically sourced materials in all our products.
